🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters To Us

Last Updated: July 2026

TinyTalent ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how TinyTalent collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application and website.

By using TinyTalent, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. About TinyTalent

TinyTalent is an educational learning platform designed for children aged 1 to 12 years. The app provides educational videos, stories, educational shorts, language learning, interactive activities, and other learning resources in a safe and engaging environment.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following information during account registration:

  • Child's Name
  • Parent or Guardian Mobile Number
  • Child's Date of Birth
  • Child's Gender

This information is used only for providing the TinyTalent service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Personalize educational content according to the child's age.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Manage subscriptions.
  • Send important service-related notifications.
  • Maintain the security of our platform.

4. Educational Video Content

TinyTalent provides access to educational videos available through YouTube using the official YouTube player.

TinyTalent does not own or host third-party YouTube videos.

All copyrights and intellectual property rights remain with their respective content owners.

Your use of YouTube content may also be subject to Google's and YouTube's applicable Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

5. Subscription & Payments

TinyTalent offers optional premium subscription plans.

For Android users, subscription purchases are securely processed through Google Play Billing.

Where purchases are made through TinyTalent's official website (if applicable), payments are processed through authorized payment providers.

TinyTalent does not store:

  • Credit Card Numbers
  • Debit Card Numbers
  • CVV Numbers
  • UPI PINs
  • Internet Banking Passwords

Payment information is handled securely by the respective payment provider.

6. Children's Privacy

TinyTalent is intended for children aged 1 to 12 years and is designed to be used under the guidance or supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our educational services.

Parents or guardians are responsible for managing their child's account and subscription.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Information Sharing

TinyTalent does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information may only be shared:

  • To provide requested services.
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating the app.
  • To process payments.
  • When required by applicable law.
  • To protect the security and integrity of TinyTalent.

9. Third-Party Services

TinyTalent may use trusted third-party services to provide certain features, including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • YouTube
  • Firebase (where applicable)
  • Authorized payment providers (where applicable)

These services operate according to their own privacy policies.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

11. Account Deletion

Users can permanently delete their account from within the TinyTalent application.

After account deletion, personal information will be deleted or retained only where required by applicable law.

If you need assistance, you may also contact us at: info@tinytalent.in

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The latest version will always be available on our website.

Continued use of TinyTalent after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
