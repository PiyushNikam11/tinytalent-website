🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Is Important To Us

Learn how TinyTalent collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TinyTalent. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe learning experience for children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TinyTalent mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

To create and access a TinyTalent account, we may collect:

  • Full Name (Mandatory)
  • Mobile Number (Mandatory)

These details are required for account creation and authentication.

3. OTP Verification

TinyTalent uses One-Time Password (OTP) verification to confirm ownership of your mobile number and improve account security.

OTP messages may be sent through authorized communication providers.

4. Subscription & Payment Information

TinyTalent may offer premium subscriptions that provide access to additional educational content and features.

Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways such as Razorpay.

TinyTalent does not store:

  • Debit Card Numbers
  • Credit Card Numbers
  • CVV Details
  • UPI PINs
  • Banking Passwords

5. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Verify users through OTP authentication
  • Provide educational services
  • Process premium subscriptions
  • Improve app functionality and security
  • Provide customer support

6. Children's Privacy

TinyTalent is designed for children aged 1 to 12 years.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise their child's use of the platform.

We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from children beyond what is required for account functionality and educational services.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, and disclosure.

8. Data Sharing

TinyTalent does not sell or rent personal information.

Information may only be shared:

  • When required by law
  • For payment processing
  • To provide app services
  • To protect platform security

9. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting our support team.

10. Policy Updates

TinyTalent may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be published on this page.
