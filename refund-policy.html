Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Please read our Refund & Cancellation Policy carefully before purchasing any subscription or premium services through TinyTalent.

❌

No Refund Policy

TinyTalent provides immediate access to premium digital educational content, learning materials, videos, and premium features upon successful payment.

Due to the nature of digital products and instant access to premium content, all subscription purchases and premium payments made through TinyTalent are final and non-refundable.

No Refund Policy

Once payment has been successfully processed and premium access has been activated, TinyTalent does not provide refunds, cancellations, exchanges, or credits under any circumstances except where required by applicable law.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Partial or non-usage of subscription
  • Accidental purchase by the user
  • Failure to cancel before renewal
  • Device compatibility issues
  • Internet connectivity issues
  • User inactivity
  • Personal reasons for discontinuing service

Subscription Cancellation

Users may choose not to renew future subscription periods. Cancellation of future renewals does not entitle the user to a refund for payments already processed.

Previously completed payments remain non-refundable.

Premium Content Access

Access to premium educational content, videos, learning activities, stories, and advanced features is activated immediately after successful payment, making all purchases final.

Exceptional Circumstances

Refund requests may only be considered if:

  • Required by applicable law
  • Duplicate payment due to verified technical error
  • Unauthorized transaction verified by payment provider

All requests are subject to review and approval.

Payment Security

All payments are securely processed through authorized payment gateways such as Razorpay.

TinyTalent does not store sensitive card details, banking passwords, CVV numbers, or UPI PINs.
