Delete Your Account

At TinyTalent, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion using the following method:

  • Send an email to our support team
  • Include your registered phone number
  • We may verify your identity to protect your account security
  • We will process your request manually
📩 Email: info@tinytalent.in

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, DOB, phone)
  • Uploaded content and activity data
  • App usage history linked to your account

Data We May Retain

  • Records required for legal compliance
  • Transaction history (if applicable)

Processing Time

We process all deletion requests within 7–30 days.

Once processed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

You will receive a confirmation after your account is deleted.
